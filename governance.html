Business governance

Among the most important success factors for global economic institutions are the application of policies and procedures, best administrative practices, outstanding performance, management of relationships with environmental resources, planning of group resources and the development of systematic dispute settlement mechanisms in order to avoid disputes , And that is through the business environment governance policy and that approach adopted by the group by separating ownership from management in all companies of the Arab Group on Sustainable Investment , This is to seek to build a comprehensive system governed by the policy of transparency and integrity in all stages of the work of the group's companies . We seek by building the group's institutional system by building an effective and continuous relationship with the supply networks of the group's company activities governed by specific administrative procedures and standards that are not subject to any personal whims or influence from the group's shareholders ,Through a well-defined relationship consistent with the standards of transparency and integrity in all stages of dealing with the supply networks of the group companies , Through the group's endeavor to complete the effective system of inter-communal relations with the business community through the relationship of the group's companies with the network of distributors and the recipient of the group's services through policies and procedures determined by the Manual on Policies and Procedures for Group Activities and Product Pricing Systems And services provided by group companies .
